
Scorpion Solitaire uses one deck (52 cards). You have 7 tableau piles.
Three rows of 7 cards are dealt - the first 4 cards face down and the next
3 face up in each row. Then four more rows of 7 cards are dealt, all face up.
The remaining 3 cards are put face down as a reserve.


The object of the game
To build four sequences down in suit within the tableau, each running from
King to Ace.
